## The Mantra

> **How you move through your work is what you build.**

This is Conway's Law for a single person. The quality of presence you bring to each increment shapes the shape of what emerges — not just the code, but the decisions, the architecture, the soul of the thing you're making.

Stage Manager exists to help you move well.

---

## The Daily Practice

Every session begins with the same four questions. Not as a form to fill. As a ritual to enter the work with intention.

**Gratitude**
Name one thing you're grateful for in this work right now.
*(This is not soft. This is the discipline that puts you above the line before the first line of code.)*

**One Thing**
What is the single most important thing to accomplish in this session?

**What I Need**
What context, clarity, or resource do I need to do it well?

**What I Want**
What does done feel like? What does it make possible?

These four questions are the opening move. Stage Manager holds them. The work unfolds from them.

## The Four Modes

**Setting** — Framing. Establishing the container that makes good design possible.
The constraints, the context, the project scope that give your probes somewhere to land.
Without a container, you're throwing sparks into open air.

*In Stage Manager, Setting emerges through the practice itself — the ritual questions, your design document, the project context you bring to each session. You are already doing it.*

**Sensing** — Open, receptive. Noticing what wants to emerge.
Sparks, observations, inspirations, questions.

**Shaping** — Curious, expansive. Holding complexity without forcing convergence.
Exploring the problem space. Considering options. Keeping the soul intact.

**Staging** — Structured, incremental. Building with intention.
Story by story. Script by script. Prompt by prompt.

These modes are not sequential. They are a circular dance — each loop up the mountain deepens your Setting, sharpens your Sensing, expands your Shaping, and refines your Staging.

The practice is knowing which mode you're in — and moving between them with awareness.

*Stage Manager focuses on Sensing → Shaping → Staging. Deeper support for Setting and community wisdom is coming.*
